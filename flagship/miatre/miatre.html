FAQs

  • What is mode of conduct for this event?

    The event will be held physically at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology.

  • What are the languages for elocution?

    This event is held only in Marathi as a tribute to Great Speaker P.K. Atre.

  • What are the criteria to participate in this event?

    This event open for all the students of any colleges and schools.

  • What are the topics for the event?

    १) भारतीय शेती : पारंपरिकतेकडून अपारंपारिकतेकडे वाटचाल
    २) क्रिप्टोकरन्सी : आर्थिक आणि सामाजिक वास्तव व परिणाम
    ३) सोशल मिडिया, तरूण आणि बदलती मानसिकता
    ४) वर्तमानकाळात संतांच्या विचारांची गरज
    ५) जागतिक तापमानवाढ आणि आपले प्रयत्न

  • What will be the time per candidate?

    Round 1 : 3+1 min
    Round 2: 5 +2 min

  • Will introduction time will be considered?

    Yes,it will be counted in your total time.
