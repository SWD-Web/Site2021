About The Event

About The Event

'Aatmabodh' is a digital literacy program offered to senior citizens and housewives since 2008. Users are taught the basics of computers, smartphones, government apps, MS office, and such technological education.
Even the word 'Aatmabodh' spurs our minds to action.

Our Mission

Introduce technological advancements

Educate women and seniors citizens

Make a positive impact
